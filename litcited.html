Information

Here you can find the sources we used to bring the information on this website to you.

It's important to include a proper literature cited on anything you publish. It gives proper credit to the original authors of the information you are using, and it helps the reader find more detailed information about your topic.

We use the APA (American Psychologists Association) standard for citations. You can find information on APA format here; though, any standard format is fine, as long as it follows the rules of who you're writing for, and whoever is publishing you.
